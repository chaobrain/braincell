braincell.channel module#

Calcium Channels#

CalciumChannel

Base class for Calcium ion channel.

ICaN_IS2008

The calcium-activated non-selective cation channel model proposed by (Inoue & Strowbridge, 2008) [2]_.

ICaT_HM1992

The low-threshold T-type calcium current model proposed by (Huguenard & McCormick, 1992) [1]_.

ICaT_HP1992

The low-threshold T-type calcium current model for thalamic reticular nucleus proposed by (Huguenard & Prince, 1992) [1]_.

ICaHT_HM1992

The high-threshold T-type calcium current model proposed by (Huguenard & McCormick, 1992) [1]_.

ICaHT_Re1993

The high-threshold T-type calcium current model proposed by (Reuveni, et al., 1993) [1]_.

ICaL_IS2008

The L-type calcium channel model proposed by (Inoue & Strowbridge, 2008) [1]_.

ICav12_Ma2020

: model from Evans et al 2013, transferred from GENESIS to NEURON by Beining et al (2016), "A novel comprehensive and consistent electrophysiologcal model of dentate granule cells" : also added Calcium dependent inactivation

ICav13_Ma2020

: model from Evans et al 2013, transferred from GENESIS to NEURON by Beining et al (2016), "A novel comprehensive and consistent electrophysiologcal model of dentate granule cells" : also added Calcium dependent inactivation

ICav23_Ma2020

Ca R-type channel with medium threshold for activation.

ICav31_Ma2020

Low threshold calcium current Cerebellum Purkinje Cell Model.

ICaGrc_Ma2020

Cerebellum Granule Cell Model.

Leakage Channels#

LeakageChannel

IL

The leakage channel current.

Potassium Channels#

PotassiumChannel

Base class for potassium channel dynamics.

IK_p4_markov

The delayed rectifier potassium channel of \(p^4\) current which described with first-order Markov chain.

IKDR_Ba2002

The delayed rectifier potassium channel current.

IK_TM1991

The potassium channel described by (Traub and Miles, 1991) [1]_.

IK_HH1952

The potassium channel described by Hodgkin–Huxley model [1]_.

IKA_p4q_ss

The rapidly inactivating Potassium channel of \(p^4q\) current which described with steady-state format.

IKA1_HM1992

The rapidly inactivating Potassium channel (IA1) model proposed by (Huguenard & McCormick, 1992) [2]_.

IKA2_HM1992

The rapidly inactivating Potassium channel (IA2) model proposed by (Huguenard & McCormick, 1992) [2]_.

IKK2_pq_ss

The slowly inactivating Potassium channel of \(pq\) current which described with steady-state format.

IKK2A_HM1992

The slowly inactivating Potassium channel (IK2a) model proposed by (Huguenard & McCormick, 1992) [2]_.

IKK2B_HM1992

The slowly inactivating Potassium channel (IK2b) model proposed by (Huguenard & McCormick, 1992) [2]_.

IKNI_Ya1989

A slow non-inactivating K+ current described by Yamada et al. (1989) [1]_.

IK_Leak

The potassium leak channel current.

IKv11_Ak2007

TITLE Voltage-gated low threshold potassium current from Kv1 subunits

IKv34_Ma2020

: HH TEA-sensitive Purkinje potassium current : Created 8/5/02 - nwg

IKv43_Ma2020

TITLE Cerebellum Granule Cell Model

IKM_Grc_Ma2020

TITLE Cerebellum Granule Cell Model
